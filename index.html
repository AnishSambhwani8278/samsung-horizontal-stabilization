Stabilized Camera Recorder

How to Use

1. Tap Start to open the camera. If you face any weird issues, please reload the site once

2. Hold your phone in the horizontal or vertical direction you want (keep tilt within 45°–60°), then tap Sync to set the starting angle.

3. Rotate your phone left or right to auto-rotate the video frame.

4. Tap the dropdown menu to switch between front and back camera.

5. Tap Record to start recording the rotated video.

6. Tap Stop Recording to save the video.

7. Tap Stop when finished.
